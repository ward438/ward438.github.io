Robert's Biography

This is a photo of my wife, children, and I.

My name is Robert Ward. I am in my mid to late 30's. I am married to a beautiful lady by the name of Samantha. I have to wonderfuly beautiful kiddos, ages 4 and 7. I was born in West Germany and lived there as a child and was raised primarily in south Texas in the Corpus Christi area. I joined the army in my 20s and was stationed in Schweinfurt, Germany. It was a fantastic city sitting smack dab in the middle of Bavaria. I couldn't have asked for a better garrison. In 2008 I deployed to the Wasit Province of Iraq near Al Kut on FOB Delta. Super fun... I honorably discharged shortly after return to Germany after the deployment. It wasn't long until I met my wife. We met while I was living on South Padre Island. We moved to Kentucky for a short time and were married shortly after. Not going to get into the details, but we had two wonderful children after that! We moved to Baton Rouge, LA after some time but found it lacking as a place to raise a family. So off to east Texas we went. My wife's family is from this area and here we are.

I was an artillery mechanic in the Army and moved into heavy equipment repair for the better part of ten years. It was a decent career, but it took too much of a toll on my physical well-being. I transitioned careers over to GTAW pipe welding. Also a decent choice. But tig welding in fixed positions for long hours was equally taxing on my body. Not too mention the 10-12 hour, 6 day a week minimum schedule. I decided to transition once more to a new career. Software development is the best choice I can think of. So here I am!

I enjoy playing pc games, xbox, hanging out with my wife and children and playing guitar. I am starting to develope a nice collection of guitars now. I have a nice Epiphone GS 400 Pro, old Ibanez dreadnought acoustic, Taylor 114E Grand Symphonym, and the gem of them all... my Taylor GS Mini Koa Plus. Anyway, this was fun. Thanks for reading.
